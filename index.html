  • This is a two players dice game:
  • first to reach 50 or more points wins
  • Players take turn- on each turn player rolls six-sided dice
  • If the player rolls 1, then the player gets no new points
    and it becomes other player’s turn
  • If the player rolls 2 through to 6, he/she can:
    • Roll again;
    • Hold- at this point the sum of all rolls is added to the
      player’s score and it becomes the other player’s turn;
